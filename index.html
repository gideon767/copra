About Us

Copra Trading & Logistics Ventures, is a family-owned Agro processing company founded in 2020. We specialize in transforming agro commodities into wholesome and nourishing plant-based snacks and beverages.
Our mission is to cater to the nutritional requirements of families by providing them with healthy and delicious food options. With a commitment to quality and sustainability, we ensure that our products are made from the finest ingredients while supporting local farmers. At Copra Trading and Logistics Ventures, we strive to promote a healthier lifestyle and contribute to the well-being of individuals and communities.

Our Story

Our story began with our concern about our diet as a young couple when we started raising kids. We made a family decision to embark on a journey to provide healthy, plant-based food options. Fueled by our passion for nutrition, wellness, and a desire to make a positive impact, we established Copra Trading & Logistics Ventures in 2020. Our mission is to meet the nutrient needs of consumers and make healthy food more accessible.

Our Purpose

United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 2 - Ending Food Hunger

At Copra Trading, our purpose is closely aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 2 (SDG 2) - to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture. We are committed to creating a world where access to nutritious food is a fundamental right for everyone.
